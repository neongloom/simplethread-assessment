Calculating reimbursement amounts for sets of projects

Click 'run scenario' to calculate the total reimbursement for a set of projects and visualize a daily timeline of the set.

Cell legend for the timeline:

  • green cells are reimbursed at the low cost city rate
  • red cells are reimbursed at the high cost city rate
  • the bottom row displays the reimbursement cost for each day
    • cells with a solid horizontal bar are reimbursed at the full day rate
    • cells with no solid horizontal bar are reimbursed at the travel day rate
